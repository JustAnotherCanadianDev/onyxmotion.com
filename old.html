A Digital Coach For The Modern Age

Onyx Motion is building digital coaches to help you play like an experienced athlete. The app uses smartwatches to show you how you play and how to improve at the moment you need it most. Our coach is ready to play when you are. Does learning have to be frustrating?

LEARN MORE
How it works
Start with the why
Onyx Motion tech focuses on technique: metrics, angles and speeds. The first step to fixing your game is find out finding out why.
Instant Feedback
Finding out how you did after the game is too late. Get what you need it, when you need it. Tips pop up into the stream as you play. Hit the problem, keep going.
AI x You
AI doesn’t forget. AI doesn’t get emotional. AI doesn’t get tired. AI combines long term insights with a human touch to figure out how to build the perfect coach for you.
Connect to Your Heroes
Get world class insights, tips, videos and data from your sports heroes. Their edge is at your command, right there on the court.
